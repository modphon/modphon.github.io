What does 'Mod Phon' mean? Is it an abbreviation?

'Mod Phon' is an abbreviation of 'Modulational Phonology'.

What does 'Modulational Phonology' mean?

Please don't ask. It is a very, very long story.

Why is there a space in 'Mod Phon'? Why isn't it written 'ModPhon', as one word (e.g., like modphon.com)?

The person who typed the application to become a corporation put a space there. After that it would have been too much bother to change it. True story.
